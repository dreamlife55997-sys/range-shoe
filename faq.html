FAQ

Shipping & Delivery

We offer free standard shipping on all orders over ₹4,999. For orders below this threshold, a standard flat fee applies. Standard delivery takes 5-7 business days, while express delivery takes 2-3 business days.
Currently, RANGE ships only within India. We are working closely with our parent logistics network under ELVION GLOBAL to expand international delivery in the near future.

Returns & Exchanges

We offer a 30-day hassle-free return window. If your shoes do not fit, or you are not completely satisfied, you can initiate a return directly from your account page, and our courier partner will pick up the package free of cost. The items must be unworn and in their original packaging.
Once our warehouse receives and inspects the return (usually within 3-5 days of pickup), we will initiate the refund. Bank transfers and card refunds take 5-7 business days to reflect in your account. UPI refunds are completed almost instantly.

Size & Fit

All shoes listed on RANGE are in UK sizing format. We provide a comprehensive Size Guide on our product detail pages to help you translate from US or EU standards.
Yes, most of our models are designed to fit true to size. If a specific shoe fits slightly tighter (e.g. narrow sports shoes), we will clearly note this on the product page and recommend buying half a size up.

Orders & Payments

We accept all major credit/debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, RuPay), UPI (Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm), Net Banking, and select wallets. Paying online grants you an extra 5% off at checkout.
Orders can be cancelled or modified within 1 hour of placing them. You can manage your cancellations directly in your Orders panel under your Profile, or contact our customer support line immediately.
